Clickable & Selection

States

Overlay-link pattern, interactive hover/active states, multi-select, disabled.

Hover & Click

Lifts on hover, press to activate

Selected

Ring + tinted background

Disabled

No interaction, reduced opacity

Overlay-link pattern: Add <a class="card__overlay-link"> to make the entire card clickable. Inner buttons/links stay independently clickable via z-index stacking. This is the production pattern for product cards, article cards, and any card that needs a primary action without wrapping everything in an anchor.

Accessibility

A11y

Cards with proper focus, semantics, and forced-color support.

Overlay Link .card__overlay-link gets :focus-visible outline. Inner interactive elements have z-index: 1 and remain keyboard-accessible.
Heading Hierarchy Use .card__title with appropriate heading level (h3/h4). Never skip levels. Card title appears in document outline.
Forced Colors All variants get visible ButtonText border. Selection state uses Highlight. Ghost/flat get GrayText.
Screen Readers Overlay link needs aria-label describing the action. Use <article> for standalone cards, <section> for grouped content.
