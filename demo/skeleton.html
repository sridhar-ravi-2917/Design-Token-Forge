Accessibility

A11y

Skeleton is a decorative loading indicator. It uses aria-hidden or role="status".

Non-interactive Skeleton is display-only. pointer-events: none and user-select: none prevent interaction.
Forced Colors In Windows High Contrast, a visible border with GrayText replaces the shimmer gradient.
Reduced Motion Shimmer/pulse animations are disabled. A static low-opacity fill communicates loading state.
Screen Readers Use aria-busy="true" on the container and aria-hidden="true" on individual skeletons.

<Skeleton variant="circle" size="large" />

  <Skeleton variant="text" style={{ width: '75%' }} />

<DtfSkeleton variant="circle" size="large" />

  <DtfSkeleton variant="text" style="width: 75%" />
</DtfSkeletonGroup>

<div class="skeleton" data-variant="circle" data-size="large"></div>

  <div class="skeleton" data-variant="text"></div>
  <div class="skeleton" data-variant="text" style="width:75%"></div>

  --skel-shimmer-via: rgba(255,255,255,0.3);
  --skel-animation-duration: 2s;
  --skel-radius-text: var(--radius-md);
